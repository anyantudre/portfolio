Participated in the **Oxford Machine Learning Summer School (OxML)** in the AI for Global Goals track. Theoretical foundations of ML from statistical methods and optimisation to representation learning and Generative AI.

## Topics Covered

- Theoretical foundations of machine learning
- Statistical methods and optimization
- Representation learning
- Generative AI and modern architectures
- AI applications for sustainable development goals

## Focus Areas

The program emphasized applying cutting-edge ML techniques to address global challenges including:
- Climate change and environmental sustainability
- Healthcare access in underserved regions
- Education and language accessibility
- Agricultural optimization
