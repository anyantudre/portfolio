## About
Presented alongside **Aristide BANDAOGO**, CEO of GO AI Corporation, this webinar explored how to prepare your career for the AI revolution.

## Topics I Covered

### 1. Conceptual Clarification
- Brief history of AI (from 1943 to today)
- Key terms: Data Science, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, AI
- Types of AI and evolution

### 2. Boom Factors of AI
According to Fei-Fei Li, three powerful forces inaugurated the modern AI era:
- **Explosion of data (Big Data)**: Data is the fuel of AI
- **Increased computing power**: GPUs, TPUs, cloud computing
- **Algorithm advances**: Deep Learning breakthrough in 2012, Transformers

### 3. Data & AI Careers
- Data Engineer, Data Miner, Data Scientist
- ML Engineer, Deep Learning Engineer
- NLP Engineer, Computer Vision Engineer, GenAI Engineer
- Data/BI Analyst

### 4. AI Opportunities
Multi-sector applications:
- Digital biology, climate technology, agriculture
- Robotics, transportation, logistics
- Education

### 5. In-Demand Jobs by 2030
Key insights:
- Software development jobs down ~35% in 5 years
- Entry-level positions disappearing: 50% could be automated in 5 years
- Senior AI/ML, cybersecurity roles exploding
- Engineering jobs expected to grow ~17% by 2033

### 6. Skills to Develop
**According to industry leaders:**

**Jensen Huang (NVIDIA CEO):**
> "If I were a student today, the first thing I'd do is learn AI... How to interact with ChatGPT, Gemini Pro, Grok."

**Sam Altman (OpenAI CEO):**
- Resilience & adaptability
- Speed of learning
- Creativity in problem-solving
- Mastery of tools, especially AI
- Code, for thinking and creating

**Yann LeCun (Meta Chief AI Scientist):**
- Focus on fundamental disciplines: applied math, physics, computer science basics
- Avoid short-lived technical skills

### 7. The Job Market in 2030
- Massive growth in frontline roles (agricultural workers, delivery, construction)
- Rise of care and education jobs mastering AI tools
- Growth in AI, robotics, renewable energy specialists
- Decline of repetitive tasks (cashiers, admin assistants, graphic designers)
