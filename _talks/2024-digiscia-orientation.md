## About
I had the honor of participating as a panelist in a webinar organized by **DigiScia**, alongside Fidèle YONLI and Wend-kouni Dieudonné KABRE, moderated by Phil Jacq OUEDRAOGO. The event aimed to guide future African talents towards careers in digital and IT fields.

## My Intervention
My presentation focused on **Data and AI careers**, presenting key roles such as Data Engineer, Data Scientist, Data/BI Analyst, Machine Learning Engineer etc...

I emphasized:
- Responsibilities and required qualifications for each role
- Training opportunities available in West Africa and Morocco
- Free and paid platforms for learning

## Target Audience
- **New high school graduates** looking to start a career in tech
- **Students in preparatory classes** considering computer science
- **Professionals** seeking to transition into IT careers
