Awarded the **AMCI Scholarship**, a merit-based grant of approximately $4,500 USD covering tuition and living expenses for 5 years of engineering studies in Morocco.

## About AMCI

The Agence Marocaine de Coopération Internationale (AMCI) provides scholarships to outstanding international students to pursue higher education in Morocco, fostering South-South cooperation and academic excellence.

## Coverage

- Full tuition fees
- Monthly living stipend
- Duration: 5 years (engineering cycle)
