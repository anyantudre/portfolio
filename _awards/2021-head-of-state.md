Received the **Head of State's Award of Excellence** from the Presidency of Burkina Faso for ranking **4th nationwide** out of 154,775 candidates in the Baccalaureate examination (Serie D - Sciences stream).

## Achievement

- **National Rank**: 4th out of 154,775 candidates
- **Stream**: Serie D (Mathematics and Sciences)
- **Recognition**: Personal congratulations from the President of Burkina Faso
