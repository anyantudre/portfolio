Led a team to **2nd place** at the **DataTour 2024 - Pan-African Data Science Nations Cup**, a major competition featuring over 100 teams from across Africa.

## The Challenge

- Solve Africa-centric problems in energy and sales forecasting
- Apply machine learning to real-world African datasets
- Demonstrate both technical skills and problem-solving ability

## Our Approach

- Extensive exploratory data analysis
- Ensemble modeling techniques
- Feature engineering based on domain knowledge

## Impact

This competition highlighted the importance of data science in addressing African challenges and fostered collaboration among data scientists across the continent.
