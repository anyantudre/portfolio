I'm a final-year MEng student in **Data Engineering and Artificial Intelligence** at [ENSA Safi](https://ensas.uca.ma/), with a strong foundation in applied mathematics and computer science. Currently, I work as a **Machine Learning Engineer** at [GO AI Corporation](https://goaicorporation.org/), where I design and deploy end-to-end data and AI systems.

My research interests focus on **deep learning**, **computer vision**, **NLP**, and especially **speech and language technologies for low-resource languages**. I'm passionate about building AI solutions that can make a real impact, particularly for underserved communities.
