Led a **3-week intensive Data Science & AI bootcamp** for **120+ learners** at GO AI Corporation.

## Bootcamp Curriculum

### Week 1: Python & Data Fundamentals
- Python programming essentials
- NumPy and Pandas for data manipulation
- Data visualization with Matplotlib and Seaborn

### Week 2: Machine Learning
- Supervised learning: regression and classification
- Model evaluation and validation
- Feature engineering and selection
- Scikit-learn hands-on labs

### Week 3: Deep Learning & Projects
- Introduction to neural networks
- PyTorch fundamentals
- Capstone project development
- Presentations and feedback

## Impact

Trained the next generation of data scientists in Burkina Faso and West Africa, many of whom have gone on to pursue careers in tech or continue advanced studies in AI.
